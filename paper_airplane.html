Paper Airplane

Learn how to make a simple paper airplane. It's a fun and easy craft that flies well.

Steps to Make a Paper Airplane:

  1. Take a piece of A4 paper.
  2. Fold the paper in half lengthwise.
  3. Unfold the paper and fold the top two corners to the center crease.
  4. Fold the peak down to the bottom edge of the paper.
  5. Fold the top two corners to the center crease again.
  6. Fold the paper in half along the original center crease.
  7. Fold the wings down so the top edges meet the bottom edge of the body.
  8. Your paper airplane is ready to fly!
