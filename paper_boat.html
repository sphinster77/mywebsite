How to Fold a Paper Boat

  1. Take a rectangular piece of paper.
  2. Fold it in half from top to bottom.
  3. Fold the top corners down to the center line.
  4. Fold the bottom edge up on both sides.
  5. Open the paper and fold it into a diamond shape.
  6. Fold the bottom corners up to the top point.
  7. Open the paper again and pull the sides out to form the boat.
