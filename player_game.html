Pong

P1 (left): W / S  |  P2 (right): ↑ / ↓  |  First to 7 wins!
On touch: drag your finger on your half of the screen.

Tank Battle

P1 (red): W/A/S/D + F to shoot  |  P2 (blue): ↑←↓→ + L to shoot  |  First to 5 kills wins!

Tic Tac Toe

First to 3 round wins takes the match!

Football

P1 (red): W/A/S/D  |  P2 (blue): ↑←↓→  |  First to 5 goals wins!

Checkers

P1 (red) moves up  |  P2 (blue) moves down  |  Click a piece then click a square to move. Captures are mandatory. Kings can move backwards!

UNO

P1 (red hand) & P2 (blue hand) take turns — pass the screen between turns!  |  Match color or number  |  First to empty their hand wins!
Tap a card to select, tap again to play. Tap the draw pile to draw.
